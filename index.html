Fisher-Man

A new climbing experience

Soon on itch.io!

v

Armed only with his faithful fishing rod and his wits, a lone fisherman defies the royalty and an unforgiving tower in an epic quest for the ocean's stolen fish!

Fisher-Man is a new platformer climbing game, with a unique movement system: the only way to move your character is by using his fishing rod as a grappling hook!

Levels are procedurally generated using the wave-function collapse technique

Play on the regular mode, and climb each section, fight the bosses, until you reach the very top of the tower.

Or play on speed run mode, with no enemies, and challenge your friends to climb as fast as possible! Share your exploits on social media!
